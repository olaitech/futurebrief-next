Privacy & Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to ensure basic functionality and to improve the user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

Necessary cookies

Necessary cookies are required for the website to function properly. These cookies enable core features such as navigation, security, and language preferences. The website cannot function correctly without these cookies.

Functional cookies

Functional cookies allow the website to remember your choices and preferences (such as language selection) in order to provide a more personalized experience.

No third-party tracking

This website does not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking technologies. Any analytics or measurements, if used, are handled with respect for user privacy.

Your choices

You can choose whether to accept functional cookies when you visit the site. You may also disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how the site functions.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy or cookie usage, please contact us via the information provided on the website.
